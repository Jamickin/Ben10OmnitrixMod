[b][u]BEN 10 MOD[/u][/b]

Turn into classic Ben 10 aliens using an Omnitrix-style transformation system!

[b]v0.1.0 - Bones Release[/b]
This is an early "framework" build - the transformation system is fully functional, but alien art and unique per-alien abilities are still in progress.

FEATURES:
- Hold the Transform key to open the alien selection wheel, release to confirm
- Energy meter limits how long you can stay transformed
- Cooldown after reverting before you can transform again
- Only one alien form active at a time
- The classic 10: Heatblast, Four Arms, XLR8, Diamondhead, Grey Matter, Stinkfly, Ripjaws, Upgrade, Ghostfreak, Wildmutt

COMING LATER:
- Full alien art and animations
- Unique ability kits per alien
- Longer cinematic transformation sequences
- More aliens beyond the original 10
